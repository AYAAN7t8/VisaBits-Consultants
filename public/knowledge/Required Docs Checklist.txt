Required Documents Checklist

For Students:
Valid passport (at least 3 months validity beyond return date)
Completed visa application form
Recent passport-sized photographs (2 copies)
Student letter from university/college
Last 4 months' bank statements (minimum balance £2,000)
Travel itinerary (flight and hotel bookings)
Travel insurance
Cover letter
Accommodation proof
Sponsor letter (if applicable)

For Employees:
Valid passport (at least 3 months validity beyond return date)
Completed visa application form
Recent passport-sized photographs (2 copies)
Employment letter covering last 4 months
Last 4 months' payslips
Last 4 months' bank statements (minimum balance £2,000)
Travel itinerary (flight and hotel bookings)
Travel insurance
Cover letter
Accommodation proof
Sponsor letter (if applicable)

For Self-Employed:
Valid passport (at least 3 months validity beyond return date)
Completed visa application form
Recent passport-sized photographs (2 copies)
Business registration certificate
Last 4 months' bank statements (minimum balance £2,000)
Last 4 months' business bank statements
Travel itinerary (flight and hotel bookings)
Travel insurance
Cover letter
Accommodation proof
Financial Requirement:
Minimum closing balance: £2,000
Last 4 months' bank statements required
