FAQs

Q: What is a Schengen visa?
A: A Schengen visa allows you to travel to any of the 27 Schengen member countries for up to 90 days within a 180-day period.
Q: How long does the visa process take?
A: Schengen visas typically take 15-30 days. We recommend applying 4-6 weeks before your planned travel date. Germany 
appointments are usually available within 3-5 weeks.
Q: What's your success rate?
A: Our success rate is 98% for Schengen and European visa applications.
Q: Do you guarantee visa approval?
A: We only guarantee approval for Germany Visit Visa applications. For all other countries, we provide expert guidance but 
cannot guarantee approval.
Q: Do I need an appointment?
A: Yes, embassy appointments are required for most visa applications. We handle the appointment booking for you.
Document Questions
Q: What documents do I need?
A: Required documents vary by visa type. The most common are:
Valid passport
Application form
Photos
Travel itinerary
Hotel bookings
Travel insurance
Bank statements
Employment/Student verification
Q: How much money do I need to show?
A: For Schengen: £50-£80 per day of stay (minimum £2,000 balance). For UK: £100-£150 per day of stay.
Q: Can I apply without a confirmed flight booking?
A: Yes, we can provide hotel and flight reservation letters for visa applications without you actually booking them.
Q: What happens if my visa is refused?
A: If your visa is refused:
Germany: Full refund of service fee
Other Schengen: No refund of service fee
We can assist with reapplying
